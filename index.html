About

Ian Adams is an experienced SDET with a zeal for front-end web development and well-thought-out user experiences. He loves taking on projects that expand the frontiers of his knowledge and skill, and he recently went back to school because he thought learning chemistry and physics would be fun.

He’s a staunch advocate of web apps being developed using automated build systems with the tests written first, and thinks AngularJS is the way of the future.

Take a look around. If you like what you see, go ahead and contact him — he’d love to hear from you. While you’re at it, give his résumé a download.
